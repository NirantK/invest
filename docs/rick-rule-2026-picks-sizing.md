## Position Sizing Framework

No fixed % template. Three sleeves with different sizing logic:

| Sleeve | Role | Sizing logic |
|---|---|---|
| Savings (gold, FNV, WPM, RGLD) | Wealth preservation | Larger, long-duration |
| Liquidity (USD cash) | Dry powder | Regime-dependent; ~40% in 2026 |
| Speculation (juniors) | Asymmetric 5–10x bets | Small % of risk capital only |

### Five Hard Rules

1. **Hours rule** — limit positions to hours/month you'll work the book. Retail = single-digit names.
2. **Risk capital only** — junior position = money you can lose 100%.
3. **Free-carry trim** — sell 25–33% when parabolic ("paid too much, too fast"), let rest ride risk-free. Called "point of no concern."
4. **No leverage** — no margin in junior mining; survive normal vol.
5. **10-bagger math** — expect high failure rate; one 10x amortizes many -30% losers.

### Time Horizon

- Most 10-baggers take 5–6 years.
- Size to survive that hold; don't over-rebalance.

Durrett (Rule-adjacent): 8–12 high-conviction junior names max.
