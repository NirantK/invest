## Key Takeaway

- **If you want pure data center / infrastructure REIT exposure:** SRVR is the cleaner choice, albeit with a slightly higher expense ratio (~0.60% vs 0.50%).
- **If you want data center REITs + digital infrastructure tech (semis, networking):** DTCR gives broader exposure including NVDA, AVGO, MRVL in the tail.
- **VPN** is only relevant for non-US investors needing a UCITS wrapper.

Both DTCR and SRVR are heavily concentrated in Equinix (EQIX) and Digital Realty (DLR) as top positions. If you already hold those individually, overlap would be significant.
